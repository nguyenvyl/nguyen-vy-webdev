About Me

Student • Cartoonist • Dev in Training

Hi, my name is Vy Nguyen. I'm a master's student in computer science at Northeastern University's Seattle campus.

My Background

I spent 4 years of my life completing a BS in biology with a minor in art.

During my undergrad, I spent over a year interning at various pharmaceutical research companies, including Takeda Pharmaceuticals and Sanofi-Genzyme Corporation. My time there made one thing clear: biology (and most fields) needed more programmers. Traditional research and healthcare are often legions behind in technology. Integrating a little computer science into these fields, I found, could allow people to spend less time wrangling technology and more time on their jobs.

So, I entered Northeastern University's ALIGN program, designed for students without a programming background to obtain a master's degree in CS.

Present

I'm in the second year of my master's program, set to graduate in late 2017. I'm also currently employed at Group Health Cooperative. I'm loving computer science so far!
